Personal Bio-Protective Mask

Protect the first-line doctors

A full facepiece N99 respirator providing whole-face, all-in-one protection, eyes, nose and mouth, for care providers working in highly contagious environments.

Why It Matters

Designed for extreme biohazard conditions

Treating seriously ill, highly infectious patients puts care providers in close proximity to a dangerous environment, especially during procedures like intubation, extubation, bronchoscopy and tracheal lavage.

Reports from China, Italy and the US during the COVID-19 pandemic indicated infection rates of 10–20% among front-line care providers, disabling high-value human resources and threatening already worn-down staff.

  • Whole-face N99 protection: eyes, nose and mouth, all-in-one
  • Designed for operating in extreme biohazard conditions
  • Reduces exposure risk to infective particles during short-term exposures
In Practice

Improved protection where it counts

The TopBioShield mask provides the care provider and the medical team improved protection in highly contagious environments, from emergency departments to intensive care units.
