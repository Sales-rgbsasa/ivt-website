Technological Innovations

Three technologies. One integrated approach.

Used together, TopClosure®, regulated negative pressure therapy and CITA introduce a groundbreaking approach to wound management, with the potential to redefine early wound closure across a spectrum of surgical applications.

⇤⇥

TopClosure® TRS

The Tension Relief System leverages stress relaxation and mechanical creep to harness the skin's viscoelastic properties, ensuring optimal tension management and enhanced closure of high-tension, difficult-to-heal wounds.

O₂

ROINPT

Regulated Oxygen-Enriched and Irrigation-Negative Pressure-Assisted Wound Treatment integrates oxygen to combat anaerobic infections while providing hydrodynamic wound cleansing that enhances the healing process.

✚

CITA

Controlled In-Situ Targeted Ultra-High Concentration Antibiotic Administration revolutionizes antimicrobial delivery, combating localized, resistant infections by delivering antibiotics directly at ultra-high concentrations.

The CITA Methodology

Ultra-high concentration antibiotics, delivered exactly where they're needed

Spearheaded by our CEO, Moris Topaz, MD, PhD, the CITA methodology evolved from over 15 years of rigorous basic science and clinical research. It enables direct delivery of antibiotics to the infection site at ultra-high concentrations, aiming to overcome resistance mechanisms and maximize treatment efficacy.

  • Direct in-situ administration via local instillation or catheter-based delivery
  • Minimizes systemic antibiotic exposure and potential side effects
  • Effective against biofilms and high bacterial loads that conventional methods cannot address
  • Published in leading journals including JACC
