Your Data, Respected

Privacy policy

IVT Medical Ltd. respects your privacy. This page explains what information this website collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Contact form details: when you submit our contact form, we receive the name, email address, phone number and message you choose to provide.
  • Basic technical data: our hosting provider processes standard server information (such as IP address) as required to deliver the website securely.
  • Aggregated usage statistics: we may measure anonymous, aggregated site usage (such as page views and country of origin) using privacy-friendly analytics that do not identify individual visitors and do not use tracking cookies.

What we do not do

  • We do not use advertising or tracking cookies
  • We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information
  • We do not send marketing emails without your consent

How we use your information

Details submitted through the contact form are used solely to respond to your inquiry, provide requested materials (such as brochures or technical documentation) and follow up on your request. Form submissions are delivered to us by our hosting provider (Netlify) and stored securely.

Data retention and your rights

We keep inquiry details only as long as needed to handle your request and maintain our business relationship. You may ask us at any time to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you by contacting us at the address below.

Third-party links

Our Research pages link to external scientific publishers (such as journals and PubMed). These websites operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.
