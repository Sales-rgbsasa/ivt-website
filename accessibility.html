Our Commitment

Accessibility statement

IVT Medical Ltd. is committed to making its website accessible to people with disabilities, in accordance with Israeli Standard 5568 and WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines.

Accessibility features on this website

  • Full keyboard navigation, including a "Skip to main content" link and visible focus indicators
  • Alternative text descriptions for meaningful images
  • Consistent, semantic heading structure and page landmarks
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Support for the operating system's "reduce motion" preference: animations are disabled for users who request it
  • Responsive design that supports zooming up to 200% and all screen sizes
  • Forms with clear labels and error prevention

Content warning mechanism

The Clinical Cases page contains graphic medical imagery and is protected by a content warning that requires explicit confirmation before viewing. This mechanism is fully keyboard accessible.

Known limitations

Some linked resources (external scientific publications and PDF documents) are hosted by third parties and may not be fully accessible. If you encounter difficulty accessing any content, please contact us and we will provide the information in an alternative format.

Feedback and contact

If you experience any accessibility issue on this website, or need information in an alternative format, please contact our accessibility coordinator:
