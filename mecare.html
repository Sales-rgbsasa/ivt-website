Portable NPWT

Regulated negative pressure therapy, anywhere care happens

MeCare α® brings ROINPT-compatible wound therapy to both hospital and home use, enhancing patient mobility and extending advanced wound care into home, rural and low-resource settings.

The Device

Compact. Regulated. Built for real life.

The MeCare α® is designed to provide pre-set, regulated negative pressure to open, small to medium size wounds for home and hospital applications.

It may promote wound healing through growth of granulation tissue and by removal of excess exudates, irrigation fluids and infectious materials.

  • Chronic, acute, traumatic, sub-acute and dehisced wounds
  • Small partial-thickness burns
  • Ulcers, diabetic or pressure
  • Flaps and grafts
ROI-NPT Compatible

Enhanced healing with oxygen and irrigation

Regulated, vacuum-assisted treatment by MeCare α® can be improved by combining the administration of low-flow oxygen and irrigation to the wound (regulated oxygen-enriched and irrigation negative pressure treatment; ROI-NPT).

This combination helps prevent and reduce anaerobic infection and accelerates the evacuation of bacteria, enhancing wound healing where conventional therapy falls short.
